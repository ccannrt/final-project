Reimaging National Park Service Trip Planning

Product Design

  • Timeline: 9/2023 - 12/2023
  • Solo UX Designer

The the National Park Service website, NPS.gov, is the hub for all official information for National Parks. It is the first places many prospective park visitors will get information about parks: Where to go, what to do, when they should go, where to park, accessibility info, etc.. As a result, nps.gov serves a wide range of users, from teenage to retired, all 50 states and international, and folks of all interests, backgrounds, and abilities.
